    Handwritten Text Recognition using AI

      Made with:

  • Utilization of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for Feature Extraction: Leveraged Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to extract meaningful features from images of handwritten documents. CNNs were used to capture spatial hierarchies in the data, allowing the model to understand the visual characteristics of different handwriting styles.
